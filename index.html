D&D Minis by Justin

Welcome to The Wizard's Craft! This website is a gallery of tabletop miniatures. Here you will find pictures of WIPs, finished miniatures, scatter terrain, and terrain builds that I have created since November of 2019. You will mainly find painted examples of 28 mm tabletop miniatures. However, I have a diverse interest in all types of miniatures. I will also be working on crafting terrain boards, tiles, scatter terrain pieces from XPS foam. I will be using LED lights in some of my future projects as well. Check out my blog to stay up to date on what I am working on currently and my links to follow my other panels I am using. Thank you for stopping by and checking out my work!
